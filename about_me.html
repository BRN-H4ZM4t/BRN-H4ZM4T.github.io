I started drawing in 2020 during the beginning of the pandemic. I needed something to put my mind to and dedicated my self to learning how to draw while keeping up with my studies the best I could while online. My very first car drawing was a Nissan GTR that I drew after a year of practice. The year prior I was in a battle bot team in the 8th grade. I was in charge of coding and co-leader of design. Our bot only made it one round into the tournament but it's something I'm very proud about doing at a young age.
