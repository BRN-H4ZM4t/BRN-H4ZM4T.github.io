In the shadows of a shattered star,
  Where ancient battles leave their scar,
The Arbiter walks, a solemn knight,
 With honor forged in endless fight.


His armor gleams, a ghostly sheen,
 A warrior’s path, both fierce and keen.
Once a foe, now a beacon bright,
 He stands for truth, in darkest night.
Betrayed by pride, his kin’s despair,
He rises up, with strength to bear.


For duty calls, and vengeance fades,
 In unity, a new hope wades.
Among the ruins of a lost empire,
 He seeks redemption, kindled fire.
A leader born from ashes’ grace,
With every step, he finds his place.


So let the stars bear witness true,
 To the Arbiter’s path, in shades of blue.
In the endless war where shadows dwell,
 He fights for peace—his heart’s own spell.
